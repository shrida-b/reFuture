    the importance of recycling

    What is recycling?

    • Recycling: Turning waste into reusable material

    How does it help?

    • The amount of waste we create leads to being more sustainable.

    impacts of recycling

    • Saves yards of landfill space
    • Conserves energy
    • Raises overall environmental consciousness
    • Reduces pollution
    • Saves money!

    review

    • Sustainability means creating the least waste possible and only using what we need in order to meet our basic necessities.
    • One way of doing this is through recycling, which is the process of turning waste into reusable materials.
    • Ultimately, recycling saves natural resources, keeping the environment healthier, while saving money and energy, keeping humans happier.
