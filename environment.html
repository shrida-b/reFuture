    • the issue

    • Environmental sustainability is concerned with whether environmental resources will be protected and maintained for future generations.
    • Issues of environmental sustainability include long-term health of ecosystems, renewable resources, preventing the consequences of man-made global warming, the protection of species diversity and ecological structure, and more.

    the importance of environmental sustainability

    What is environmental sustainability?

    • Responsibly interacting with the planet to maintain natural resources & avoiding jeopardizing the ability for future generations to meet their needs

    Why is it important to be sustainable?

    • Reduces waste and pollution to the earth
    • Less harmful to the ecosystems on Earth
    • Ensures in the future we have enough resources to meet our needs

    goals of environmental sustainability

    Shifting to renewable sources:

    • Limiting consumption of non-renewable sources

    To do this

    • Reuse what you can
    • Don’t use single-use plastics

    Protecting the health of ecosystems:

    • Avoid damaging ecosystems to the point where they can’t be repaired to ensure in the future we have enough resources

    To do this

    • live a sustainable lifestyle!

    Avoiding excess pollution:

    • Reduce waste as pollution damages our health and Earth’s atmosphere

    To do this

    • Reduce waste such as food, products
    • Prevent pollution (ex: choose to walk rather than drive)

    Protecting species diversity:

    • Protect species diversity and the ecological structures they live in to maintain the current food chain and benefit innovation

    To do this

    • Don't litter when you're outside
    • Don't damage natural ecosystems
