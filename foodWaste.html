    • the issue

    • We generate 21.5 million tons of food waste each year.
    • If we composted that food, it would reduce the same amount of greenhouse gas as taking 2 million cars off the road.

    the importance of not wasting food

    what is food waste?

    • Food that is not eaten and gets thrown away

    why is not wasting food important?

    • Reduces methane emissions from landfills
    • Prevents pollution involved in selling food
    • Conserves energy and resources

    how to stop wasting food (compost)

    What is composting?

    • The decomposition of organic material ( things that were once living in nature) that can be used as fertilizer.
    • Ex: turning old fruits into new fertilizers, in order to grow plants

    How to compost

    • Use your city's compost systems
    • Make your own compost at home with a bin of dirt mixed with twigs and leaves

    what you can compost

    • Food scraps including egg shells, meat, & bones
    • Food-soiled paper, napkins, paper towels, pizza boxes, cardboard egg cartons, non-plastic take out containers, & paper plates
    • Coffee grounds, tea bags, & yard waste
    • Compostable packaging

    impacts of saving food & composting

    • No wasted freshwater & groundwater resources
    • Reduced production of methane
    • Better land usage
    • Reduces the need for chemical fertilizers
    • Promotes higher yield of crops
    • Pulls carbon out of atmosphere

    review

    • There is a large amount of food waste created every year
    • This is harmful because it wastes natural resources and produces harmful gasses in landfills
    • We must prevent it by composting our excess food and making new fertilizer to grow plants
    • Ultimately, composting saves food, while keeping the environment healthier, and individuals happier
