    • Weekly Planner

    Don't know how to begin a sustainable lifestyle? Here are some different activites you can try out in the span of a week that will get you into the habit of trying new sustainabe acts!

    Day 1

    REUSABLE BAGS: Try using resusable bags when you shop at any store. Most stores sell their own but if you don't have any, here are some places you can purchase some to take with you on your shopping journeys!

    Custom Eco Friendly Bags sells a variety of affordable bags and Eco Bags sells different styles of bags to choose from as well.
    Activity has been completed!

    Day 2

    RESEARCH: Take 30 min to check out these vegan and eco friendly self care and cleaning products!

    Preserve-- Seventh Generation --Love Goodly --EcoTools
    Activity has been completed!

    Day 3

    CLEAN UP: Cleaning around and outside the house is a nice freshresher and can be even better when you recycle and compost your trash.

    Take 20-30 minutes to pick up any plastic items around your living space and toss them into a recycling bin!

    Activity has been completed!

    Day 4

    WATCH AND LEARN: Need a break from a busy week but still want to learn about sustainablility and our planet? Here are some documentaries you can watch!

  • -Chasing Coral (2017)
  • -Our Planet (2019)
  • -There's Something In the Water (2019)
  • -When Two Worlds Collide (2016)
  • -Rotten (2019)
  • -Minimalism (2016)
    • Activity has been completed!

    Day 5

    PLANT THE DAYS: Want to set an eco-friendly atmosphere around your livin space? Try planting or caring for some plants! Plants release oxygen, make pretty green spaces, and can feed us.

    Some beginner plants include: Peperomias, succulents, kale, mint, and spider plants.

    Activity has been completed!

    Day 6

    GREEN BASKET: Want some accessories and overall sustainable fashion options? Take a few minutes to check out these websites...

    PelaVisionSells Sustainable phone cases at medium-high prices. Etiko sells vegan clothing and shoes with organic cotton. Patagonia is a more well known brand that focuses on eviromental ethics with their different clothing for active and casual wear. Verloop sells clothing, living, and repurposed items from using fabric scraps and excess materials.

    Another cool place to check out are your local thriftstores! Sometimes the best and most affordable places to buy are from second hand shops. Always amazing to put those finds to use and wear them in your own way.

    Activity has been completed!

    Day 7

    FOOTPRINT REFLECTION: Want to end your week off with a reflection? Calculate your ecological footprint! It is important to understand how much ecological resources we use up in our households and everyday lives.

  • A good calculator for this is on the Global Footprint Network site. It calculates your data and provides information on each topic and what you can do for our planet.

    Activity has been completed!

    • So why plan?

    This plan includes suggestions on how easy it is to incorporate sustainability into our lives in just one week. Planning is a good way to keep track of these actions and encourage each user to reflect on their habits.

    Planning is also a good way to help with time management and simply writing down what happens along your week allows you to sit down and take a mental break from life. Not only can making a green planner (whether it be on a notepad or recycled paper) take a positve toll on our planet, but it can positively impact our mental health too. Let's change our lives one day at a time!

    End of a new day...! Now I encourage you as a user to use what you read and learned from our educational and lifestyle page to come up with activities on your own! Plant your days!
