    • the issue

    • Fast fashion uses cheap, toxic textile dyes, synthetic materials, and poor agricultural practices that all negatively impact the Earth.
    • Fast fashion is the second largest water polluter after agriculture.

    the importance of sustainable fashion

    Why is sustainable fashion important?

    • Sustainable fashion is the production of clothes, shoes, and accessories in environmentally and socio-economically sustainable manners.
    • Sustainable fashion pollutes the Earth less.

    the importance of responsible consumerism

    While buying sustainably sourced clothing is important, responsible consumerism is just as important.

    What exactly is responsible consumerism?

    • Responsible consumerism, or ethical consumerism, is when customers make intentional purchases of products, opting for those that have minimal to no negative impact on humans, animals, and the environment.

    What does it mean to be a responsible consumer?

    • buy what you need
    • Recycle, reuse, and repair as much as you can
    • Try not to waste anything (applies to more than just clothes)
