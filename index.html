    • Why you should use reFuture.

      Our earth is deteriorating at a faster rate than ever before. In fact, according to unep.org, the extraction and processing of materials, fuels and food (all things YOU consume!) contribute half of total global greenhouse gas emissions and over 90 percent of biodiversity loss and water stress. That's a horrible statistic, if you ask us, and it's only getting worse. Resource extraction has more than tripled since 1970, and by 2060, global material use could double to 190 billion tonnes (from 92 billion). But we can change this.

    • What we offer to our users.

      Our website serves not only as a one-stop-shop for learning about sustainable practices but also helps you become more sustainable through various activities. To get started, we recommend you visit the quiz page to evaluate your knowledge of sustainability and receive tailored advice on where to begin your journey in our garden of information. However, if you would like to explore our resources yourself, you can visit our education page to learn more about sustainable practices and the lifestyle or activities page to put those sustainable practices into action.
